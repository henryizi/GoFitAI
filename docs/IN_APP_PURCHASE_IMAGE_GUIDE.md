## ğŸ“¤ How to Upload in App Store Connect

### Step 1: Navigate to In-App Purchase

1. **Go to App Store Connect**: https://appstoreconnect.apple.com
2. **My Apps** â†’ **GoFitAI**
3. **Features** â†’ **In-App Purchases**
4. **Click on `gofitai_premium_lifetime1`**

### Step 2: Upload Image

1. Scroll to **"App Store Promotion"** section
2. Find **"Promotional Image"** or **"Product Image"**
3. Click **"Choose File"** or **"Upload"**
4. Select your 1024x1024 image
5. Click **"Save"**

### Step 3: Enable App Store Promotion (Optional)

1. In the same section, toggle **"Enable App Store Promotion"**
2. This allows the image to appear on your app's product page
3. Click **"Save"**
